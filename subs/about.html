MARHABA! THIS IS “AMAD”


Learn More

"AMAD" which stands for "ASK ME ABOUT DUBAI" Is an Online Travel and Tourism Website that focuses on show casing all the travel attractions in Dubai. Call us your travel partners around the “Desert of wonders” Dubai.
Travel and tourism being at the heart of Dubai, it's more efficient once there is sufficient information in place to aid tourists when making travel decisions. Here at AMAD we take pride in pointing you in the direction of the most luxurious hotels, flight offers and tourist attractions. We not only stop at that but bring to you the latest deals and promotions on almost everything around town.
Yalla!! Come lets explore the desert full of unimaginable wonders...

Our Vision

To make travel information readily available to everyone that needs it at zero cost.

Our Mission

To build long term relationships with our clients through providing exceptional services.

Core value

We believe in serving our clients with integrity and honesty.

Scope of work

Our Service is for general information purposes. It allows you to browse through various hotels, latest flight offers and the latest shopping deals allover Dubai. Feel free to check out any to know more and equip yourself with the required information for making informed travel and shopping decisions.
